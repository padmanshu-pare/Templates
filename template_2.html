About Me

Once upon a time, I moved to a small city with a suitcase and a camera. I learned that the best parts of life are the little routines — morning tea on the balcony, the corner bookstore, and conversations that last too long. I bake on Sundays, photograph sunsets in the evenings, and write letters sometimes.

This is a short snapshot — I prefer experiences over lists of achievements.

My Philosophy

I believe in curiosity, kindness, and slow living. I try to learn something small every day and use what I learn to help people around me. Quality of life matters more than quantity of tasks.
